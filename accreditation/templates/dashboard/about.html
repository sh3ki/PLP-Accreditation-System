About Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Pasig

Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Pasig

Excellence in Education, Service to the Community

Our History

Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Pasig (PLP) is a premier local university in Pasig City, Metro Manila, Philippines. Established to provide quality and affordable education to the youth of Pasig and neighboring cities, PLP has grown to become a center of academic excellence and community development.

Academic Programs

PLP offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs across various colleges, including:

  • College of Business and Accountancy
  • College of Computer Studies
  • College of International Hospitality Management
  • College of Engineering
  • College of Education
  • College of Arts and Sciences
  • College of Nursing

Accreditation Commitment

PLP is committed to maintaining the highest standards of quality education through continuous improvement and accreditation. Our Accreditation Management System ensures that all programs meet national and international standards, providing our students with world-class education and globally competitive skills.

Our Achievements

PLP has consistently produced graduates who excel in their chosen fields, contributing to the development of Pasig City and the nation. Our commitment to excellence has earned us recognition from various accrediting bodies and educational institutions.
