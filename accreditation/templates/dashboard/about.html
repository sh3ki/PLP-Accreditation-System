About Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Pasig

Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Pasig

Brief History

Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Pasig (PLP) was established on June 21, 1999, through City Ordinance No. 27, Series of 1999, under the administration of Mayor Vicente P. Eusebio. The university was created to provide affordable and quality higher education to the constituents of Pasig City and neighboring communities. Starting with just a few programs, PLP has evolved into a comprehensive university offering various undergraduate and graduate programs across multiple disciplines. The institution continues to uphold its commitment to academic excellence, research, and community service.

Vision

An empowered Pasig Community through excellent education focused on future-proofed student success and improved quality of life anchored on the common good in a multicultural world.

Mission

To provide an inclusive and transformative education that empowers students to succeed in an ever-changing world. Through community engagement, we foster a culturally diverse and globally aware learning environment equipping students with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary for total human development.

Core Values

  • Academic Excellence - Commitment to the highest standards of teaching, learning, and scholarship.
  • Cultural Identity - Preservation and promotion of our rich cultural heritage and local traditions.
  • Peace Education - Fostering a culture of peace, understanding, and harmonious coexistence.
  • Sustainable Development - Dedication to environmental stewardship and sustainable practices.
  • Social Equity and Responsibility - Promoting fairness, justice, and active community engagement.
  • Global Competitiveness - Preparing students to excel in the international arena while maintaining local relevance.
