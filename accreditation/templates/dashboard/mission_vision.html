Mission & Vision

VISION

Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Pasig envisions itself as a premier local university that provides quality and accessible education, producing globally competitive and socially responsible graduates who contribute to the sustainable development of Pasig City and the nation.

Global Competitiveness
Social Responsibility
Sustainable Development

MISSION

Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Pasig is committed to:

  • Provide quality, relevant, and affordable education to the youth of Pasig City and neighboring areas
  • Develop competent and morally upright professionals equipped with knowledge, skills, and values
  • Promote research, innovation, and extension services for community development
  • Foster a culture of excellence, integrity, and social responsibility
  • Collaborate with various stakeholders to address societal needs and challenges

CORE VALUES

Excellence

Striving for the highest standards in all endeavors

Integrity

Upholding honesty, ethics, and moral principles

Service

Commitment to serve the community and society

Innovation

Embracing creativity and continuous improvement

Accountability

Taking responsibility for actions and outcomes

Compassion

Showing empathy and care for others
